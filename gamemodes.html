Gamemodes

Besides standard difficulty there are 3 additional difficulties.

Hard mode

After defeating the Dubious Magia you unlock hard mode buffing all enemies giving them more health and bosses new moves. Hard mode does stack on the other gamemode.

X mode

To unlock X mode you must do the “Sword Master Quest” where you go to the shrine of left and accept the Quest where you must travel all the way to the shrine of right without casting a single spell & defeat the boss fight in the “shrine of right” to complete the quest and unlock the “X Sword” & gain the togglable X mode.

Requiem mode

To unlock Requiem mode you must collect “The Broken Viola” located behind a destroyable wall at the start of the game “The Broken Bo” located in the alley of the damned and place them at the altar at the peak, where you unlock “the strings of the dead” & gain the togglable Requiem mode.
