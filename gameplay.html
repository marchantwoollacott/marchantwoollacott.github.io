gameplay

The game is made with a keyboard in mind so your mouse is not needed. Your set movement options are WASD, SHIFT (Sprint) & SPACE BAR (Attack) . Your SPACE BAR is linked to your blade The inventory is set to T & the magic meter refresh set to R.   

Magic has two primary features - The Magic Meter & Magic Pages.

the magic meter

The Magic Meter will be drawn if you use too many spells. You have to wait for the bar to refresh before casting a new spell.

Magic pages

At the beginning of the game you have three slots for Magic Pages. These slots only fit one of many spells you can choose from. You have a large number of combinations that you can make.
